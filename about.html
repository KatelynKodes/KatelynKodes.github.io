My name is Katelyn, I'm a 21 year old woman who lives in the south. I'm a graduate of Academy Of Interactive Entertainment (AIE) and have an Associate of Occupational Studies in Game Programming. Oddly enough though, my journey into game development did not with me wanting to tell games, but rather, tell stories.

When I was in highschool I had originally intended to become an illustrator. However, I found as I carried on into senior year that just showing people my illustrations wasn't enough, I wanted people to know and understand the stories behind the things I created, I just wasn't entirely sure how to do so.

That was until I discovered Ren'py, a visual novel engine. making a visual novel was something I entertained for a while so as a teenager with nothing much to do I decided to give it a shot. Next thing I know, I created and finished my first visual novel, Project 4th Wall, and it recieved some engagement. I began to post smaller games under the alias "JellioGushi" for that small period of time.

As I learned I started to entertain the idea of game development more and more, and now here I am! Feel free to take a look at my projects page to see what I have accomplished since then. I now no longer use the alias of JellioGushi for my professional work.
