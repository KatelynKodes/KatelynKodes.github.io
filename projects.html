    B-Force

    January 2023 - June 2023

    B-Force is a game where you play as a bee in a suburban backyard, the objective of the game is to race around a track for a total of 3 laps in as less time as possible.

    My responsibilities in this project consisted of UI functionality as well as the placement and functionality of checkpoints the player must pass through in order to complete each lap.

    Frigid Peak

    April 2022 - June 2023

    Frigid Peak is a game where you play as a hiker trying to reach the top of a snowy mountain. The goal of the game is to reach the topmost platform while avoiding snowballs being thrown by a yeti at the top of the mountain.

    My responsibilities on this project consisted of player movement and some UI functionality.

    Project 4th Wall

    June 2017 - November 2018

    Project 4th Wall was my first game. It was a visual novel where the player is invited onto a test project to bring two androids to life. The game had branching route options and some minigames the player could play to progress the story.

    My responsibilities on this project were mostly everything, I created all the art and programmed the game from scratch using the visual novel engine Renpy. The only thing I did not create was the music. I sourced the music from Eric Matyas' library of copyright free music, SoundImage.org.
